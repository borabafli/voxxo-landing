Privacy Policy

Effective date: 13 May 2026 · Last updated: 13 May 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Lorem Software ("Lorem Software", "we", "us") collects, uses, stores, and protects information when you use the Voxxo mobile application ("Voxxo", the "App"). Voxxo is an AI-powered voice notes app that lets you record audio, transcribe it to text, generate AI summaries, translate notes, and (optionally) sync them across devices.

We designed Voxxo to be privacy-first: most of your data lives on your device. Cloud features are optional and only used when you sign in or subscribe to Voxxo Pro.

Quick summary: We collect only what's necessary to make Voxxo work. We do not sell your data. We do not use your voice recordings or notes to train any AI model. You can delete your account and all associated data at any time from inside the App.

2. Information we collect

2.1 Information you provide

  • Account information (only if you sign in): email address, display name, and an account identifier issued by Apple if you use Sign in with Apple. Apple Sign-In may return a private relay email; we use whatever email you choose to share.
  • Profile preferences: language, default speech-to-text provider, and other in-app settings.
  • Content you create: voice recordings, transcripts, translated text, AI summaries, folders, tags, and any text you type into a note.
  • Support messages you send to info@loremsoftware.com.

2.2 Information collected automatically

  • Subscription status from RevenueCat (whether you have an active Voxxo Pro entitlement, plan type, renewal status). We do not receive your full payment card or banking details — those stay with Apple.
  • Diagnostic and crash logs (only if you have enabled Apple's "Share With App Developers" setting). These contain technical information about device model and OS version, not the content of your notes.
  • Usage counters (e.g. seconds of speech-to-text used in the current period) to enforce free-tier limits and Pro entitlements.

2.3 Information we do not collect

  • We do not access your contacts, photos, calendar, location, or browsing history.
  • We do not embed third-party advertising SDKs and we do not use the App Tracking Transparency framework, because we do not track you across other companies' apps or websites.
  • We do not collect your raw payment-method details.

4. Voice, microphone, and speech recognition

Voxxo asks for microphone and speech-recognition permissions only when you start a recording. Recording is an explicit, user-initiated action — we never listen in the background.

  • On-device transcription (default for free accounts): audio is processed by Apple's on-device speech recognition. Audio and transcripts stay on your device unless you choose to sync.
  • Cloud transcription (Voxxo Pro, optional): audio is sent over an encrypted connection to our backend (Supabase Edge Functions in the Tokyo region) and forwarded to a third-party speech-to-text provider (e.g. Deepgram). Providers process the audio only to return a transcript and do not retain it for training.
  • AI summaries / edits: when you trigger an AI feature, the relevant transcript text is sent to our AI provider (e.g. OpenAI / Anthropic) through our backend solely to generate the requested output. We instruct providers not to use your data for model training, in accordance with their enterprise terms.

5. Where your data lives

  • On your device: WatermelonDB (SQLite) stores all your notes, transcripts, and metadata locally. Audio files are stored in the App's sandboxed file system.
  • In the cloud (only if you sign in and/or sync is enabled): Supabase project hosted in Tokyo, Japan. Data is encrypted in transit (TLS 1.2+) and at rest. Row-Level Security policies ensure that one user can never read another user's data.
  • Apple: handles all in-app purchases. We never see your card details.
  • RevenueCat: stores anonymized subscription state.

6. Sharing with third parties

We share information only with the limited set of processors required to operate Voxxo:

  • Supabase (database, auth, storage, edge functions).
  • RevenueCat (subscription management).
  • Apple (Sign in with Apple, App Store payments, on-device speech recognition).
  • Speech-to-text providers (e.g. Deepgram) — Pro users only, and only when cloud STT is enabled.
  • AI providers (e.g. OpenAI, Anthropic) — only when you trigger an AI summary or edit.

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data to anyone. We never share data with advertising networks.

7. International transfers

Because Voxxo is operated from Türkiye and uses cloud providers in Japan, the United States and the EU, your data may be transferred outside your country of residence. Where required (e.g. for EU/UK users), we rely on Standard Contractual Clauses or equivalent safeguards offered by our processors.

8. How long we keep your data

  • Notes, transcripts, recordings: kept until you delete them or delete your account.
  • Subscription records: kept as long as you have a Voxxo account, plus up to 7 years for tax/accounting compliance.
  • Support emails: kept for up to 24 months after the conversation ends.
  • Diagnostic logs: kept for up to 30 days.

9. Your rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correct inaccurate data (you can edit your profile from Settings).
  • Export your data (Settings → Export All Notes).
  • Delete your account and all associated data (Settings → Delete Account, or see our Account Deletion page).
  • Withdraw consent or object to processing.
  • Lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority.

Send any request to info@loremsoftware.com. We respond within 30 days.

10. Children

Voxxo is not directed to children under 13 (or under 16 in the EU). We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe a child has used Voxxo, contact us and we will delete the data.

11. Security

We use TLS for all network traffic, row-level security in the database, scoped service tokens for backend functions, and Apple's secure enclave (via expo-secure-store) for credentials on device. No system is perfectly secure, but we follow industry best practices.

12. Changes to this policy

If we make material changes, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top and, where appropriate, surface a notice inside the App. Continued use after the change means you accept the updated policy.
